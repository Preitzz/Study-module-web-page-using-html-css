DEADLOCKS

Deadlock refers to a speific situation when 2 or more processes are Waiting for resources in a circular chain or two or more processes are waiting for each other to release a resource. A Deadlock is a situation where each of the computer process waits for a resource which is being assigned to some another process. None of the process gets executed since the resource it needs,is held by some other process which is also waiting for some other resource to be released.
BANKER'S ALGORITHM<
Banker's Algorithm is a deadlock avoidance algorithm. It is also used for deadlock detection and resource allocation . This algorithm can be used in the banking system to ensure that the bank never allocates its available cash in such a way that it can no longer meet the requirement of all of its customers.
